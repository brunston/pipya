pipya

Pipya is a personal assistant written in python. Pronounced pip-pah, it is designed to be a jack-of-all-trades partner-in-crime that sits in a terminal window. It can fetch the weather and RSS news headlines, as well as remember your name and what city you prefer your weather from. It can also process limited-natural-language commands, such as "please fetch me the weather" or "fetch me headlines from the Atlantic, please" or "who are you?".
